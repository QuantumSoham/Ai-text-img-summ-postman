AI Text Summarizer and Image Generator App

Welcome to the AI Text Summarizer and Image Generator App! This app leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence APIs to provide concise summaries of long texts and generate images from text descriptions. Whether you have a lengthy article, research paper, or any other text document that you want to summarize quickly, or if you want to visualize your text, our app can assist you.

Simply paste your text into the text area below and click the "Summarize" button to get a summary, or use the "Generate Image" button to create an image from your text.
